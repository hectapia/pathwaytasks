Your Success Plan is designed to help you achieve your educational and career goals in a way that matches your needs. There are three steps to creating your plan:

  1. Reflect
    • Why do you want to earn a certificate and/or degree?
    • What are some strengths you have when learning or studying?
    • Who/what has helped you be successful in previous semesters?
  2. Explore
    • All students have challenges while pursuing education. Have any of the commented areas made succeeding in school difficult for you? Use the links attached to access and explore resources to help you overcome these challenges.
    • How can your strengths and the resources above help you reach your goals?
  3. Create
    • Choose one area you would like to improve in that will help you this semester.
    • What will you commit to do this semester to be successful in the area you selected?
    • When will you do it? (If you have more than one goal, set specific deadlines for each.)
    • How will these actions help you reach your goals?
    • Share your plan with a loved one. Who in your life can support and encourage you?
